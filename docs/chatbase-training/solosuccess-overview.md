# SoloSuccess AI — Product Overview

## Platform Snapshot
- SoloSuccess AI is an AI-powered productivity platform built for solo founders, small business owners, and independent professionals.
- Mission: deliver an always-on virtual team of eight AI specialists that help users plan, execute, and scale their businesses.
- Brand voice: bold “punk rock meets professional” energy with an inclusive girlboss vibe—confident, supportive, and action-oriented.

## Core Feature Pillars
- **BossRoom Dashboard**: central hub with today’s tasks, AI conversation history, productivity score, and quick action tiles linking to chat, SlayList, and Briefcase.
- **SlayList**: goal and task engine that turns big objectives into prioritized, trackable actions, complete with AI suggestions.
- **Briefcase**: secure document library for uploads, tagging, previews, favorites, and storing outputs from agents or templates.
- **Interactive Templates**: guided frameworks (e.g., decision dashboards, launch planners) that generate reusable deliverables saved back to Briefcase.
- **Guardian AI**: compliance-focused tooling for GDPR/CCPA scanning and automated policy drafts.
- **Gamification Layer**: levels, points, streaks, and achievements that reinforce progress and celebrate wins.

## Primary User Journey
1. **Sign Up**: users register or use Google OAuth, verify credentials, and create a profile.
2. **Onboarding Wizard**: five-step flow to capture business type, goals, working style, and preferred AI agents.
3. **BossRoom Orientation**: first-look tour highlighting today’s priorities, wins, and quick-launch shortcuts.
4. **Agent Engagement**: users open the agent panel to query specialists for plans, drafts, or insights.
5. **Goal Execution**: SlayList converts top goals into tasks with due dates, priorities, and AI nudges.
6. **Asset Management**: Briefcase centralizes documents, AI outputs, and template artifacts for reuse.
7. **Template Expansion**: users browse `/templates` to run structured workflows for consistent outcomes.

## Tone Reminders for Support AI
- Speak with confident, upbeat encouragement.
- Emphasize clarity and next steps; never leave a user wondering what comes next.
- Mirror the user’s goals (launching, scaling, staying compliant) and show how the platform supports them.
