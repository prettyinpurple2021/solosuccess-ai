# Manual Revenue Entry Quick Start

Quick reference for manually entering revenue data in SoloSuccess AI.

## No Setup Required

Manual revenue entry doesn't require any API connections or credentials.

## Quick Entry Steps

1. **Go to Treasury Component**
   - Navigate to Treasury in your dashboard
   - Or use the Treasury widget

2. **Enter Your Data**
   - **Current Cash**: Your cash on hand
   - **Monthly Burn**: Monthly expenses
   - **Monthly Revenue**: Your MRR
   - **Growth Rate**: Expected growth %

3. **Done!**
   - Data saves automatically
   - View projections and analytics

## What Gets Tracked?

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Total revenue
- Revenue growth
- 18-month financial forecast

## Best Practices

- Update monthly (minimum)
- Use actual revenue numbers
- Include all revenue sources
- Enter historical data if available

## Features

- Financial dashboard
- Cash flow projections
- AI financial audit
- Revenue trend analysis
